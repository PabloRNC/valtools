ValTools Privacy Policy

Welcome to ValTools, a free Twitch extension designed to connect streamers with their community by displaying essential Valorant profile data. We take your privacy seriously, and this policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data We Collect

ValTools does not store any sensitive personal information. The data we handle includes:

  • Riot name, tag and puuid (Player Universally Unique IDentifiers).
  • Basic profile information such as player card, rank, and match history (agent, score, and personal stats like KDA and ACS).
  • We do not collect any sensitive data.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used solely to display information on the Twitch extension and improve your experience with ValTools. This includes:

  • Displaying Valorant profile information in an interactive overlay.
  • Providing real-time updates on match history and player performance.
  • We store data in a end to end encrypted MongoDB database and redis cache server.

3. Security and Data Protection

ValTools uses JWT (JSON Web Tokens) to secure your data. This ensures that only individuals using the extension have access to the information, protecting it from unauthorized third-party access. Additionally, streamers can choose to hide their match history if they prefer.

4. Your Control Over Data

Streamers have full control over their data displayed through ValTools. You can choose to hide your match history if desired, ensuring your gameplay details remain private. The extension does not store any sensitive information beyond what is necessary for its functionality.
