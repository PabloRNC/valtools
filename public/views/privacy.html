ValTools Privacy Policy

Welcome to ValTools, a free Twitch extension designed to connect streamers with their community by displaying essential Valorant profile data. We take your privacy seriously, and this policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data We Collect

ValTools does not store any sensitive personal information. The data we handle includes:

  • Riot name, tag, and puuid (Player Universally Unique IDentifiers).
  • Basic profile information such as player card, rank, and match history (agent, score, and personal stats like KDA and ACS).
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information for security and optimization purposes.
  • We do not collect any sensitive data.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used solely to display information on the Twitch extension and improve your experience with ValTools. This includes:

  • Displaying Valorant profile information in an interactive overlay.
  • Providing real-time updates on match history and player performance.
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of the service by monitoring technical data.
  • We store data in an end-to-end encrypted MongoDB database and Redis cache server.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

ValTools connects to Riot's official API and other Third Party APIs to collect and display relevant data. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share information with:

  • Service providers necessary for hosting and maintaining ValTools.
  • Authorities, if required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

4. Security and Data Protection

ValTools uses JWT (JSON Web Tokens) to secure your data. This ensures that only individuals using the extension have access to the information, protecting it from unauthorized third-party access. Additionally, streamers can choose to hide their match history if they prefer.

5. User Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also object to or restrict certain types of processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to servers located outside your country of residence. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is protected in compliance with relevant laws.

7. Automated Decision-Making

ValTools does not use automated decision-making processes or profiling that could significantly impact users.

8. Not endorsed by Riot Games

ValTools is an independent tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Riot Games. Riot Games is not responsible for the development, support, or functionality of this extension.

9. Legal Basis for Data Processing

We process your data based on your consent, contractual necessity, or our legitimate interests, such as maintaining security and enhancing user experience.

10. Changes to This Policy

ValTools may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. We advise you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
